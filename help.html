Changing Browser Permission Settings Manually

For Google Chrome

  1. Click the vertical elipsis options menu in the upper right-hand corner of your browser window located to the right of your avatar
  2. Click 'settings' from the dropdown menu (located near the bottom)
  3. When you reach the settings page, click on 'Privacy and security' located in the left sidebar menu. Alternatively, you can scroll down the settings page until you locate 'Privacy and security' located under 'Advanced'
  4. Click on the 'Site Settings' option
  5. Under 'Permissions', click on the 'Location' option
  6. Under the list of blocked sites, click on Event Finder's URL
  7. Under 'Permissions', click the arrow menu to the right of 'Location' and select 'Allow'

Location is now enabled for Event Finder. Using the link below, navigate back to the home page.
